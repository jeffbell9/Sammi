In Memoriam

Sammi Davis, Jr Jr

2007 - 2017

It came to me that every time
I lose a dog they take a piece
of my heart with them, and
every new dog who comes
into my life gifts me with a
piece of their heart. If I live
long enough all the components
of my heart will be dog, and
I will become as generous
and loving as they are.

We miss you, Sam
